ABOUT US


Pine City Mall is a shopping centre in Pine City, Cape Town, South Africa. The 65,000 square-metre development cost R650m to build and officially opened in September 2017. The mall is owned by South African entrepreneur and property developer Sinoxolo Keipjas under The Maponya group in partnership with Investec and ZenProp property holdings

Situated in the hub of Samora Machel, Khayelitsha the location is central for all surrounding townships. This 70 000m2 diamond layout mall is home to over a 170 shops, a vast array of flagship stores, local and international brands, and full governmental facilities that would make life easier in the community.The mall has uniquely identified court areas made for easy shopping navigation, as well as exceptional access for both motorists and walk-ins.It is a truly great place to enjoy your day, as we aspire to provide the best service for our communities. Pine City Mall is The Soul of Khayelitsha!
